About the Author

Peter Seddon is a British storyteller renowned for his witty, heartfelt tales that weave humor, nostalgia, and the quirks of everyday life into captivating narratives. With The Boomer Crew series, Seddon shines as a master of character-driven adventures, bringing the irrepressible spirit of his lovable, mischievous retirees to life with charm and authenticity. A self-published gem, his sharp dialogue and knack for blending comedy with poignant moments mark him as a standout voice in contemporary fiction. With The Boomer Crew, Peter Seddon brings his signature wit to this hilarious crime caper series featuring Britain's most unlikely would-be criminals.
